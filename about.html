About me

I’m from (and based-in) Seattle, WA and I’ve worked with computers for as long as I can remember. When I only about 11, I would use platforms like Homestead and Geocities to build websites around the things I was interested in (Dragon ball Z and beatboxing) and as I grew older, I became self-taught and proficient in HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

As a developer with an eye for good design, I have a passion for creating beautiful websites and collaborating with others to envision digital spaces that exceed their expectations.
